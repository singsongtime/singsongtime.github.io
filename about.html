Wyatt Wakefield

about me

Wyatt Wakefield (b. 1994 in Morro Bay, California) is a US-American composer and performer, who is also active in lens-focussed art forms. The scope of his work ranges from from concert music to video works, and extends to a number of ongoing projects and collaborations. He has recieved education at Temple University Philadelphia, at Kunstuniversität Graz, and at the Hochschule für Musik und Darstellende Kunst Stuttgart, while his mentors have included Matthew Greenbaum, Klaus Lang, and Jennifer Waslhe.
