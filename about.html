Wyatt Wakefield

About & Contact

Wyatt Wakefield (b. 1994 in Morro Bay, California) is a US-American music practicitioner (composer, performer, theater-musician etc.) & educator whose body of artistic work stands or falls in celebration of the arrival of a particular spark between emergent and residual tendencies within the history of European-American music, attempting to re-evaluate through aesthetic production the effects of globalisation on contemporary musical experience.

He has received education at Cuesta College San Luis Opispo (California), Temple Univerity Philadelphia, Kunstuniversität Graz (Austria) and at the Hochschule für Musik und Darstellende Kunst Stuttgart. His teachers have included Matthew Greenbaum, Klaus Lang, and Jennifer Walshe.
