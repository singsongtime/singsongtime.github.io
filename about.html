Wyatt Wakefield

about me

Wyatt Wakefield (b. 1994 in Morro Bay, California) is a US-American composer, whose body of work stands to greet the arrival of a particular spark between two poles, specifically, the specific relation between the dominant high-modernist tradition of European-American concert music and those emergent, postmodern attempts to stage something like an "expanded field" of musical thought by way of intermedial crosspollinations or conceptual inquiry.
He has recieved education at Cuesta College San Luis Opispo, Temple Univerity Philadelphia, Kunstuniversität Graz, and the Hochschule für Musik und Darstellende Kunst Stuttgart; his mentors have included Matthew Greenbaum, Klaus Lang, and Jennifer Walshe.
