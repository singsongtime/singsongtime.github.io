Wenn ich ein Vöglein wär'

Wenn ich ein Vöglein wär'
Und auch zwei Flüglein hätt',
Flöge ich zu dir.

Weil's aber nicht kann sein,
Weil's aber nicht kann sein,
Bleib' ich allhier.

— Songtext: Johann Gottfried Herder
— Year: 2020
— Performed by: Virtual ensemble of cut-together self-recordings made by Atay Bağcı, Tin Džaferović, Simon Kintopp, Tobias Kochseder, Tom Kraines, Álvaro Vallejo Larre, Rosa Ortega, Thorsten Schwarz, Milica Vujadinovic
— Location: Graz
— Comments: This music is based on the melody of the song "Wenn ich ein Vöglein wär'". I wrote a Bachian choral from the melody, then bracketed each measure to be reinterpreted as a long, swelling exhale, within which the three, upper voices together yet asynchronously loop that measure's fragmented, melodic contents above a slow, forward-moving bass line. The audio is a composite of recordings that talented musician colleagues and friends of mine made of themselves playing to the bass line -- way back in Spring at the beginning of the Covid lock-downs.

Was hat dieser Sommer...

Was hat dieser Sommer
Mit mir zu tun,
Hochherzige Gazelle?

Die Jäger
Malen den Graubäumen
Kraft, strich auf, strich ab,
der Borke belebendes Weiß.

— Songtext: Kerstin Hensel
— Year: 2020
— Performed by: Ensemble from the theater piece "Die Kette Der Ricke"
— Location: Reiterkaserne, Graz
— Comments: This music appeared as part of the theater piece "Die Kette Der Ricke" on 25.09.2020 in the Reiterkaserne, Graz. The musicians played this music from the three balconies enclosing the court yard in which the performance took place. I later processed the recording through a vinyl emulator.
